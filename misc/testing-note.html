Backstory

Arrival in Spifftopia

In search of a new beginning, Nydid arrived at the gates of Spifftopia, left in a state where power was shifting rapidly, and those without connections found themselves lost in the chaos. Penniless and weary from his long journey, Nydid sought refuge in the one place that still offered sanctuary to the downtrodden: the local church of Godking Spiff.

Here, Nydid heard the sermons of the Church's priestess, preaching the divine rule of Godking Spiff, the god who, according to legend, had blessed the lands of Spifftopia with prosperity. Captivated by the teachings and desperate for purpose, Nydid found solace in the faith of the Godking.

Joining the Church

After the sermons, Nydid remained, offering his services to the church in exchange for food and shelter. What began as simple labor soon became an integral role in the daily operations of the church. He helped maintain the church grounds, assisted in organizing the donations, and tended to the flock of believers that streamed through the temple doors.

It was during this time that he met Davelore, a fellow refugee of sorts, who had also come seeking purpose within the walls of the church. Together, the two worked tirelessly to support the church as its influence spread across Spifftopia. Their loyalty did not go unnoticed.

As the Church grew in power, more people flocked to its cause, and soon, the voice of Godking Spiff echoed far beyond the city. The Church became a central power in Spifftopia’s political landscape, often mediating disputes and consolidating its influence over the common folk and nobles alike.

Rising to Templar

Nydid and Davelore’s dedication was rewarded when they were both inducted into the Templar Order, the militant arm of the Church. Clad in the sacred armor of the templars and wielding weapons blessed in the name of Godking Spiff, Nydid swore an oath to protect the faith and enforce the Church’s laws. His duty was clear: to defend the Church from its enemies, both internal and external.

While Davelore quickly ascended the ranks, eventually becoming Templar Grandmaster, Nydid took a more humble path. Though a skilled fighter, he found a deeper calling in ensuring that the Church’s ranks were well-fed and supplied during the times of famine. His contributions were not on the battlefield but in the kitchens, where he transformed meager rations into meals that kept the Church's forces strong.

Disillusionment and Exile

However, as the years passed, Nydid began to notice cracks in the foundation of the Church he had sworn to serve. Corruption, greed, and a thirst for political power seemed to infect the upper ranks of the clergy. Once a humble institution dedicated to the worship of Godking Spiff, the Church had become a political machine, prioritizing its own gain over the well-being of the people it claimed to protect.

Nydid’s disillusionment grew as he witnessed the suffering of the common folk, ignored by a Church more focused on expanding its wealth and influence. He spoke out, urging the Church leaders to return to their true purpose: serving the people in the name of Godking Spiff. But instead of listening, they silenced him.

Branded a traitor to the Church, Nydid was stripped of his rank and exiled to a distant island, far from Spifftopia. Though the exile was presented as an act of mercy, it was clear to Nydid that it was meant to be his end. Banished from the only life he had known, he was left with nothing but his skills and his faith—though even that was shaken.

Life on the Island

The island was a harsh and desolate place, inhabited only by a few fellow exiles. Despite the isolation, Nydid found a sense of peace in the simplicity of his new life. He no longer had to navigate the treacherous politics of Spifftopia, and for the first time in years, he was free to live without fear of betrayal.

Nydid did not take on any formal roles in the small exile community. Instead, he helped wherever he could, offering his strength and skills as needed. Often, he would join expeditions into the wild, venturing into untamed lands in search of resources.

Mining for the town became one of his primary contributions. He spent hours in the dark caverns beneath the island, searching for precious minerals and materials that could help the community survive. Though the work was grueling, it gave him a sense of purpose and helped him distance himself from the political scheming of his past.

Setting Up a Stew Shop

But Nydid’s greatest contribution came not from the forge or the fields, but from the kitchen. Drawing on the skills he had honed during the Spifftopian Famines, Nydid began cooking for the community. What started as simple meals soon became a source of pride for him.

He set up a small stew shop in the inner ward, where he provided nourishing meals to the townsfolk. As he stirred the bubbling pots, Nydid found a new sense of purpose—not in battle or in service to a corrupt institution, but in the simple act of feeding those in need.
